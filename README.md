## ✨ What is this?

**Passport Photo Maker** is a lightweight, single-file web app that turns any selfie or gallery photo into a perfectly sized **3.5 × 4.5 cm Indian passport photo**, arranged neatly on an **A4 sheet** — ready to print at home or at any photo shop.

> 🎯 Just open the page, snap or upload a photo, crop it, and download a high-resolution, **600 DPI, DSLR-quality** print sheet in seconds.

## 🧭 How It Works

```
┌────────────┐    ┌─────────────┐    ┌──────────────┐    ┌───────────────┐
│  1. Upload  │ →  │  2. Adjust  │ →  │ 3. Settings   │ →  │ 4. Download    │
│  📷 / 🖼️    │    │  ✂️ Crop &  │    │ 🔢 Copies &   │    │ ⬇️ Save & Print │
│             │    │  ☀️ Brighten│    │ 📁 Format     │    │                │
└────────────┘    └─────────────┘    └──────────────┘    └───────────────┘
```

1. **Upload** — Take a photo with your camera or choose one from your gallery.
2. **Adjust** — Drag to reposition, pinch/slide to zoom, rotate, and tweak brightness until your face fits the guide.
3. **Settings** — Pick how many copies you want on the A4 sheet (8 / 12 / 16) and your preferred download format.
4. **Download** — Get a ready-to-print A4 sheet with thin cut guides around each photo.

## 🖨️ Printing Tips

> 📌 **At a print shop:** Ask for **"A4, actual size, no scaling"**
>
> 🖨️ **At home:** In your print dialog, select **"Actual Size"** or **"100%"** — never "Fit to Page"

For best results:
- Stand in front of a **plain, light-colored wall**
- Make sure your face is **well-lit and evenly lit** (avoid harsh shadows)
- Face the camera **directly**, with a **neutral expression**
- Avoid glasses, hats, or anything covering your face
